CASuDev is a locally powered conservation and poverty relief association. It is a community based, non-profit, non-political and non-governmental association working to strengthen local communities participation in the conservation of biodiversity and ecosystem functions, through measures that address the protection and wise use of natural resources, and the socio-economic and cultural development of the affected communities.

This association was created by a group of people who care about the nature, social, economic, ecological and cultural wellbeing of the underprivileged and marginalized people in the local areas. This association strives to link the economic livelihoods of the poor to the biodiversity and environmental conservation.
